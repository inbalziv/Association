Temporary Adoption Process

Temporary adoption is for a one night. It gives the dog/cat option to get a warmer environment and gives the adopter joy and simulation of having a dog/cat at home

The adopters must meet several conditions:
  • They must be over 18 years of age, living in their own home, or renting a residence that allows pets.
  • Fill the temporary adoption application with your personal details.
  • The next step is we will contact you once you approved.
  • You will have to pick up the dog/cat at 4-6pm and returns till 2pm.

Sunday-Thursday (10:00 am – 6:30 pm), Friday (8:00 am – 1:30 pm), Saturday (11:00 am – 1:30 pm)
